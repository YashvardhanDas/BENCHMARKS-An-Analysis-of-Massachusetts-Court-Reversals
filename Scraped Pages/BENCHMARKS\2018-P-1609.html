06/17/2019 #11 Motion to expedite the dismissal of appeal and to open FAR, filed for Dashaun Wright
07/01/2019 RE#11: Denied. I note that in a previous order, a single justice of this Court permitted the defendant to renew his motion to file a nonconforming brief and appendix if it was "accompanied by the proposed brief and appendix. . . on or before 5/17/19." The defendant remains free to file a motion seeking an extension of that time showing "good cause" for his failure to meet that deadline, and to submit his nonconforming brief and appendix along with a motion seeking leave to file them. To the extent the defendant intends to abandon his appeal, the court shall adhere to the timelines contained within the court's standing order concerning dismissals of appeals and reports in all cases for lack of prosecution. For information pertaining to filing an application for further appellate review, the defendant should contact the office of the Supreme Judicial Court's clerk's office for the Commonwealth. (Rubin, J.). *Notice.
