10/11/2018 RE#3: ALLOWED FORTHWITH. The petitioner's Affidavit of Indigency appears regular and complete on its face, and indicates that the petitioner is indigent. The court hereby allows the Motion to Waive entry fee. Notice Sent.
10/15/2018 RE#1: On January 27, 2014, a single justice of this Court issued an order requiring Chukwuma Azubuko to obtain permission before he may enter an appeal. The order provides for the following procedure:

"[B]efore entering any new appeal in the Appeals Court, Azubuko must file a motion, with a copy of this order, in the single justice session of this court seeking leave to docket the appeal. The Clerk of this Court is instructed not docket any appeal of Azubuko unless a single justice determines that the appeal is not frivolous and grants Azubuko leave to docket his appeal. If two (2) justices of this Court determine that the appeal is frivolous upon review of his motion, the appeal shall not be docketed." See 13-P-1903.

Treating Mr. Azubuko's motion to docket/vacate dated October 1, 2018, as a motion to docket his appeal in Suffolk Superior Court No. 1884CV01549, the motion is denied. After review of the motion and of the record assembled by the Superior Court, we conclude that the appeal is frivolous. (To the extent Mr. Azubuko seeks to vacate the single justice's 2014 order, the time for challenging that order is long past.) Mr. Azuboko is reminded that he is required to included a copy of the 2014 order with any future motions seeking permission to docket an appeal. (Massing, Henry, JJ.). *Notice/Attest
