04/26/2019 #1 Motion to docket appeal late, filed by Robert Wolsfelt.
04/26/2019 #2 Assembly of Record received from Essex Superior Court.
05/01/2019 RE#1: Because counsel avers that he did not receive notice of assembly of the record until receiving the letter from the Appeals Court's clerk's office, the motion to docket the appeal is allowed. See Mass. R. A. P. 10 (1) (a) ("Within 14 days after receiving from the clerk of the lower court the notice of assembly of the record, each appellant . . . shall pay to the clerk of the appellate court the docket fee . . . or request waiver of the fee . . . ."). The plaintiff shall pay the entry fee of $300 on or before May 15, 2019. (Blake, J.) *Notice/Attest/Frison, J.
06/19/2019 #3 Motion to extend time to pay $300.00 entry fee filed for Robert Wolsfelt by Attorney Steven Charles Goldwyn.
06/19/2019 RE#3: Allowed to 6/24/19 to pay the $300 entry fee. No further enlargement. (Blake, J.) *Notice
