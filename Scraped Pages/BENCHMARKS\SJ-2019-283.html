08/05/2019 #8 ORDER: "This matter came before the Court, Lowy, J., on the defendant's application for leave to file an interlocutory appeal pursuant to Mass. R. Crim. P. 15 (a) (2) filed on July 9, 2019. In accordance with Commonwealth v. Jordan, 469 Mass. 134 (2014) , the notice of appeal and application were timely filed. Upon consideration, it is ORDERED that the application be, and the same hereby is, allowed. The interlocutory appeal shall proceed in the Appeals Court. It is FURTHER ORDERED that the interlocutory appeal shall be CONSOLIDATED with any existing appeals filed by Gadomski's codefendants in the Appeals Court challenging the trial court's June 6, 2019 order denying the defendants' joint motion to suppress. The Criminal Clerk's Office of the Bristol Superior Court shall assemble the record in 1773CR00138 and transmit the record to the Clerk's Office of the Appeals Court, John Adams Courthouse, One Pemberton Square, Room 1-200, Boston, Massachusetts 02108-1705." (Lowy, J.)
