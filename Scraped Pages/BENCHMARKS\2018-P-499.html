09/07/2018 RE#9 (Revised): On August 2, 2018, the father filed a motion to stay a modification judgment dated January 12, 2018 and entered in Hampden Probate and Family Court docket number HD13W1953WD. On August 3, 2018, I ordered mother to file a response by August 10, 2018. No response was filed, and on August 27, 2018, mother was reminded that her response was overdue. She was given until September 4, 2018 to file a response. The mother has not filed a response to the motion to stay or a request for additional time to do so.

Based upon the motion and supporting material, the father has demonstrated a sufficient likelihood of success on the merits and of irreparable harm to him to warrant staying the modification judgment pending his appeal. Therefore, paragraphs 1 through 4, inclusive, of the modification judgment shall be stayed pending further order of this Court or a single justice thereof. The stay shall take effect on Tuesday, September 11, 2018, i.e., the modified visitation schedule shall remain in effect for the weekend of September 7, 2018-September 10, 2018. (McDonough, J.). *Notice/Attest/Wyner, J.
