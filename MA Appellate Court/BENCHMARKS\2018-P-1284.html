03/01/2019 Entry vacated as having entered in error (By the Court 3/1/19) ORDER: A review of the trial court docket shows that this appeal is from the trial court's order denying defendant's motion to suppress. The defendant filed, and the trial court allowed, a motion to enlarge time to file application for leave to appeal under rule 15(a)(2). The trial court docket does not reflect that the application was allowed, nor does a review of the SJC single justice docket show that the application has been filed, although the record was assembled and notice of assembly sent to this court and the parties . Accordingly, appellate proceedings in this matter are stayed to April 1, 2019, by when a status report is due from the defendant as to whether entry of this appeal should be vacated. *Notice/Attest/Gildea,J
