12/11/2018 ORDER: The petition is denied. In general, there is no right of appeal from the denial of a motion for summary judgment, and the petitioner has not identified any basis for recognizing an exception in this instance. See Maxwell v. AIG Domestic Claims, Inc., 460 Mass. 91, 97 98 (2011). For similar reasons, the request for single justice relief is denied. Single justice relief is reserved for truly extraordinary and exceptional cases and provided only in a "stinting manner." Edwin R. Sage Co. v. Foley, 12 Mass. App. Ct. 20, 25 (1981). After review of the petition, the supporting documentation and the judge's decision and order, I conclude that such exceptional circumstances are not presented here. (Sullivan, J.). *Notice/Attest/Lu, J.
