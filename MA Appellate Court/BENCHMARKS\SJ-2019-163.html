10/18/2019 #24 JUDGMENT: "This matter is before the court, LENK, J., on an emergency petition for extraordinary relief pursuant to G. L. c. 211, § 3, and the parties' joint status report of October 15, 2019, in response to this court's interim orders. The parties report that the two Boston Municipal Court criminal matters at issue were dismissed on the Commonwealth's motion on September 13, 2019. The parties request that this court vacate the arraignments in those cases and also order that the petitioner's criminal record, to the extent created by the "unlawful arraignments," be expunged. See Commonwealth vs. Roderick Webber, country court Docket No. SJ-2019-0366 (Sept. 9, 2019); G. L. c. 276, § 100K (a) and (a) (6); Mass. R. Crim. P. 16. See also Police Com'r of Boston v. Municipal Court of Dorchester Dist., 374 Mass. 640, 665 n. 18 (1978). Cr. Commonwealth v. Cheney, 440 Mass. 568, 574 (2003). Upon consideration of the parties' joint motion, it is ORDERED that the petition for extraordinary relief under G. L. c. 211, § 3, shall be, and hereby is, ALLOWED in part. The arraignments in Boston Municipal Court Nos. 1801CR05910 and 1907CR01353 shall be vacated and set aside. It is FURTHER ORDERED that the parties' joint motion to expunge the petitioner's criminal record created as a result of these two improper arraignments, shall be, and hereby is, ALLOWED. The clerk of the Boston Municipal Court, Central Division, shall expunge all records of the matters from its filed, and shall notify the Commissioner of Probation and the Criminal History Systems board to do so as well." (Lenk, J.)
